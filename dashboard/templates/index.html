How the pipeline works
1
RSS Ingestion
12 curated AI feeds (TechCrunch, Wired AI, OpenAI, DeepMind, etc.)
|
2
Embedding-based Deduplication
text-embedding-3-small + cosine similarity (threshold 0.85)
|
3
AI Relevance Filter
Reuses dedup embeddings to reject non-AI articles at zero extra cost
|
4
Topic Classification
NLP, Computer Vision, LLMs, Robotics, General AI
|
5
Smart Ranking + GPT-4o Curation
Source diversity, topic spread, and recency scoring before final top-10 selection
|
6
Discord Delivery
Weekly digest posted every Monday at 10 AM ET via webhook
